# Stunts

Stunts are active use powerups that help in the middle of rounds.
Default 1 item slot. Can be modified with house rule stickers and totems.

### Before you throw
- Splitshot
    - Throw 2 small balls instead of one big one
- Money shot
    - All bonus tickets earned by this ball are doubled
- Mr. Consistent
    - Throws your last shot again

### While ball is in play

- Brake
    - Kill all momentum instantly
- Nudge
    - Give ball bonus momentum ina specific direction
- Magnet
    - Pull ball towards bullseye for a second
- Ghost
    - Ball passes through one wall before turning back solid
