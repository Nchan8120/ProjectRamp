### Common Totems
- Juggler: +1 stunt item slots
- Bailout: Miss gives 1 ticket
- Ol' Reliable: The lowest scoring hole is twice as large 
- Bumper Cars: each bounce gives +100 points
- TBD: Free reroll in shop
- TBD: allows you to go in debt 
- TBD: scored ball has a 1/4 chance to generate a stunt card
- Ticket Magnet: increase bonus ticket pickup range

### Rare Totems
- Bonus Ball: +1 ball
- Safety Net: Miss is refunded as free ball and the totem is destroyed
- Clutch Gene: Last ball of the round is worth 2x
- Penny Pincher: leftover balls converted to tickets at double rate
- TBD: Bounce of 5 walls to generate an engineering bonus

### Epic Totems
- Hot Streak: each consecutive scoring throw increases a multiplier by 0.1x, resets on miss
- Copycat: copies the effect of the totem to its left
- Under Pressure: each miss increases multiplier by 0.25x , resets each round
